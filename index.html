Tip: Notes save automatically per video on this iPad. Tap a timestamp to jump there. The laser (target icon) glows red and fades 0.5s after you lift.

Presenter mode shows only the video + your ink — no controls appear, so it's clean on a projector. Pick your pen colour first, then tap Present. Control it invisibly:
• Pencil = draw  •  1-finger tap = play/pause
• 2-finger tap = pen ↔ laser  •  3-finger tap = clear
• press & hold (1 finger) or Esc = exit
A Bluetooth keyboard/clicker also works (Space = play/pause, ←/→ = seek, Z = undo, C = clear, 1–6 = colours).
